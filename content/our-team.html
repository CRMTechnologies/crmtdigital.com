Andrew Freeman

Chief Executive Officer

Andrew co-founded the company in 2000 and is responsible for the strategic direction of the business as well as maintaining close relationships with clients to ensure successful programme delivery.  He leads CRMT with a clear purpose to align clients’ sales and marketing processes to drive business revenue.
